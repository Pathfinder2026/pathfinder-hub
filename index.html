What is Pathfinder

Pathfinder is an internal Training Health & Readiness Assessment tool that makes invisible training gaps visible—helping SNUCs prepare better, Training Admins act faster, and CSMs/CSEs use training health as a leading indicator of customer success.

You are a co-creator

Pathfinder is in pilot. We're collecting requirements from internal stakeholders and customers to inform the development of a native SNU version. Your usage, feedback, and customer insights are the foundation of the business case that will bring this into SNU natively.
